Our story

Why we started fixing phones instead of selling new ones.

Our founder, Navpreet, was a hardware technician who kept seeing the same thing: people told their phone was "not worth repairing" when the real fix was a ₹700 part. NP Mobile Repair was built around one rule — never recommend a replacement when a repair will genuinely do the job.

Nine years on, that rule hasn't changed. We've repaired everything from cracked flagship screens to water-logged phones written off by other shops, and we still open every job with a free, honest diagnosis.
